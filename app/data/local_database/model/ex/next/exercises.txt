handle in one hand at shoulder height with the elbow
bent at your side.


EXECUTION

1 Squeeze the shoulder and twist your body to the
other side to press the handle diagonally up until
your arm is straight overhead.

2. Pause for a few seconds, return to the starting
position slowly and switch to the other side and
repeat the same repetitions.

KEY TIPS

1 Do not lock out your elbows at the top.

2. Keep your knees bent and feet fixed throughout
the movement.

PREPARATION
