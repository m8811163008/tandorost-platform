Bicep Curl

FOCUS AREA. Biceps, Forearms
EQUIPMENT Bodyweight

PREPARATION

Stand closely to an object with feet together. Hold
it and extend your arms to lean back with the arms
tucked to your sides.


EXECUTION

1 Bend your arm to stand up straight.
2. Lean back to the starting position slowly and
repeat.

KEY TIPS

1 Keep your chest up and eyes looking forward
throughout the movement.

2. Keep a straight back and braced core during the
exercise.

Bulgarian Split Squat


FOCUS AREA Glutes, Quadriceps, Hamstrings
EQUIPMENT Bodyweight

PREPARATION

Extend one leg behind you and place the top of your
foot on a bench behind you. Extend your arms and
place your hands by your sides.



EXECUTION

1. Inhale and slowly bend yourself down by bending
the working knee until the thigh on the same side
is parallel to the floor.
2. Exhale and raise your body back up by pushing
through your heel in a controlled manner.
3. Return to the tarting position and repeat.
4. Switch to the other side and repeat the same
repetitions.

KEY TIPS
1 Do not lean forward as you come down.
2. Do not let your front knee go over your toes.
3 Do not lock out your knees while driving up.
4 Keep a straight back and braced core during the
exercise.

Burpee


FOCUS AREA Full Body
QUIPMENT Bodyweight

PREPARATION

Stand with your feet shoulder width apart and your
arms by your sides. Push your hips backward, bend
over and lower your body into a squat position.


EXECUTION

1 Lower your torso to touch the floor with your
palms and bounce your feet backward into a
push-up position.

2 Bring knees to the chest and jump your feet
forward to the starting position.

3 Stand and jump quickly into the air and reach
arms over your head.

4 Land and bend knees to get into the starting
position and repeat.

KEY TIPS

1 Do not lift your chest first and leave the hips on.
the ground when you bring knees to the chest.

2 Keep your body straight from head to toe when in
push-up position.

3 To add difficulty, you can do the exercise with
weights.
